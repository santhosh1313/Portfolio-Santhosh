Multilingual Voice-Assisted Skin Disease Classification System

An AI-powered web system that detects skin lesions from images and explains the result back to the user by voice — in their own regional language. Built on a Swin Transformer image classifier trained on the HAM10000 and ISIC-2019 datasets, reaching 90.52% validation accuracy, paired with translation and text-to-speech for real-time, accessible diagnosis support.
